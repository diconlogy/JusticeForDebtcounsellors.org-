Mission

Mission

JusticeForDebtCounsellors.org is exactly what we stand for, justice and fairness for debt counsellors in South Africa, there needs to be a voice for this part of the industry that is not afraid to legally challenge the National Credit Regulator (NCR) and fight for the rights of debt counsellors who practice in a framework set out by the NCR that we believe have made it impossible for debt counsellors to run profitable practices, service their clients to the best of their ability and still maintain good relationships with industry stakeholders. Debt Counsellors have saved credit providers billions of rands in lost revenue and have changed the lives of millions of financially strapped consumers, yet this credit does not go to the debt counsellor but to the NCR, each year new rules and declaratory orders are imposed by the NCR on debt counsellors making it more and more difficult for the industry to serve the consumer in the best possible manner. There are approximately _____ thousand debt counsellors who have voluntary deregistered as active debt counsellors, this number supports our statement that the industry is not regulated in a fair and sustainable manner and have become a rather financial and legal risk for debt counsellors to operate in.
